Greatest Hits - Vol. 8 No. 2

There is a softcover and hardcover version to this issue.  The hardcover has 64 pages more, and is limited to 5000 copies.  The following is the index for both versions, except that the information underlined and in the color teal is just for the hardcover version.  Also the two page stories "Strips" were previously printed in January 1984 with the first pages missing.  Also the story "Slot Machine" is mostly the same as its previous printing; except that the illustrations of signs and things are left in their original language, with English lettering over it, making it harder to read; compared to the previous printing which only had the English lettering on white background.
